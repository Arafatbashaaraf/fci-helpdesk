## Policy management

Centralize all company policies so employees can access approved documents quickly.

## Steps

### Create policy entries

Add policy title, category, and effective date.

### Upload policy document

Attach the latest policy file and keep version notes.

### Announce updates

Notify employees after publishing a new or revised policy.

> [!info] Review policy owners quarterly to ensure accountability.
