## Overview

Create a professional company profile by configuring your core organization details.

## Steps to configure

### Add company basics

Update your company brand name, official email ID, custom domain name, website, and industry type.

### Link social handles

Connect official social media profiles so employees can quickly access your organization channels.

### Save and review

After updates, review all fields and save to publish your company profile information.

> [!info] You can also update these details from the company setup page in your HR portal.
