## Statutory configuration

Maintain legal and payroll compliance by updating statutory profile settings.

## Common fields

### Organization identifiers

Maintain GST, PAN, and other required registration identifiers.

### Payroll compliance

Configure PF, ESI, PT, and other local statutory options.

### Document validation

Ensure statutory documents are current and approved by finance or legal teams.

> [!warning] Incorrect statutory fields can block payroll processing.
