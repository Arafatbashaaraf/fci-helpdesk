## Department setup

Organize your company by creating clear departments and ownership.

## Steps

### Open department tab

Go to Company Profile and choose Department.

### Assign team members

Map employees to each department using the directory.

### Create sub-departments

Use Add to create nested teams and reflect real business structure.

### Save and preview

Save changes and review department hierarchy with employee counts.
