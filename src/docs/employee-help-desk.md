## Account

### How do I log in to FCI?

1. Create an account from **Sign Up**.
2. Enter your email ID and phone number.
3. Receive OTP on your registered phone.
4. Verify OTP and log in.

### How do I create my profile account in the dashboard?

1. Log in to your account.
2. Open **Dashboard**.
3. Click the profile icon.
4. Fill profile details (name, email, phone, history, education, documents).
5. Upload profile picture (optional).
6. Save changes.

### How do I access organization profile and policies?

1. Open **Company Profile** in the HR portal.
2. Select the required section:
   - Overview
   - Statutory Plans
   - Policies
   - Announcements

### How do I stay updated on announcements and policies?

1. Go to Dashboard.
2. Open **Company Profile**.
3. Use tabs:
   - Announcements
   - Policies

### How do I use employee directory and understand org structure?

- Search employees by name, department, title, or location.
- Open employee details for contact and reporting info.
- Use org chart and team views to understand structure.

## Attendance

### How do I manage and track attendance logs and requests?

1. Open **Attendance** from Dashboard.
2. View attendance logs.
3. Check approved and pending requests.
4. Review attendance analytics.

### What can I do in attendance section?

- View daily logs.
- Track requests.
- Review analytics.
- Manage corrections and requests.

### How to login/logout attendance in FCI?

1. Open Dashboard.
2. Use web clock at top corner.
3. Click once to login and again to logout.

Additional methods can include face recognition, geo fencing, geo tracking, geo tagging, biometric, and auto clocking.

### What if I forget to login or logout?

1. Notify manager or HR.
2. Go to **Attendance > Log > Select day > Get Approved**.
3. Select anomaly reason.
4. Fill exact timings and reason.
5. Wait for approval.

## Leave

### How do I manage my leaves?

1. Open **Leave** section.
2. Check available leave balance.
3. Submit leave request with type and dates.
4. Track approval status and leave history.

Common leave types:
- Sick leave
- Casual leave
- Earned/privileged leave

## Payroll

### How do I view payroll information?

1. Open **Payroll** in your dashboard.
2. Select month/pay period.
3. Review earnings, deductions, and net pay.
4. Download payslip if available.

### What if payroll details look incorrect?

1. Compare with attendance and leave records.
2. Contact HR/payroll admin with exact discrepancy.
3. Attach relevant details or screenshots.

## FCI Prime

### How do I use FCI Prime benefits?

1. Open **FCI Prime** section.
2. Browse available offers and benefits.
3. Follow offer-specific steps to claim.

> [!info] If any section is unavailable, your organization may not have enabled that module yet.
